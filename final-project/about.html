About Me








I'm Laura Clague, a marketing student studying at the University of Montana and the Davidson Honors College.














Family



Originally from Butte, Montana, I graduated high school early and started my college career at 16 years old. Highlands college of Montana Tech is where I spent my freshman and sophomore years leading up to my transfer to UM. Now living and studying at UM, I am looking to finish out my Bachelors Degree in Marketing.














Friends



With my education in Marketing, I want to run my own business. I am passionate about working towards producing sustainable and inclusive clothing that is good for the planet and people. Through the garments I want to produce, I hope to get people excited to express themselves. Getting up in the morning and having clothing that feels like your best self is the ultimate pick-me-up. I want that feeling to be universal for all bodies.
