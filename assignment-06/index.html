Spinach Bacon Quiche

  • 0.5 lbs of cubed bacon
  • 5 eggs
  • 3/4 cups whole milk
  • 8 oz spinach
  • 1 8inch pie shell
  • spices to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Cook cubed bacon over medium heat until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs, whole milk, and spices in a large bowl until evenly combined.
  4. Combine bacon and spinach with the egg mixture.
  5. Pour final combined mixture into pie shell.
  6. Place in oven and cook for 50mins or until egg is set.
